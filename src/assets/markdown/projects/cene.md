**CENE** is a ticketing platform for theatrical and cinema events.
Users can browse upcoming releases, select exact seats, leave reviews, and
download PDF tickets with scannable QR codes.

The work focused on a reliable purchase flow, straightforward event discovery,
and a polished Stripe-backed checkout experience.
